RTS/RPG

The Golden Age of Morgave Eight

Let's make a G.A.M.E. in which everyone can play their own role with full immersion in a dark and mysterious fantasy world in the best traditions of the genre. Located on a single realm, settled in such a way that each player can find a comfortable complexity and style of play, from visionary and meditative observation like in "Journey" to aggresive PVP griefing and ganking. The game world in which it will be difficult to find a difference between a human and AI, in which you can have many characters, but only one life for each of them. A game in which your playing time will be monetized and unobtrusively limited. Truly ambitious massive multiplayer real time strategy role playing game by gamers for gamers.

The Morgave Eight is a precursor of the G.A.M.E. The main purpose of the project is to establish global sandbox circuits, mechanics, economics, balance and gameplay. Since pixel graphics are much less expensive in production, on its basis it is worth starting to implement the main game concepts in to blockchain. At the end of the development of the G.A.M.E, Morgave Eight will remain integrated into it as a lower level of game interpretation. Generally you can play the PC and the mobile version of Morgave, staying in the same world.
